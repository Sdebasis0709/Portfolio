Credit Card Defaulter

A credit card defaulter project typically refers to a data analysis or machine learning project that focuses on predicting whether a credit card holder is likely to default on their credit card payments. These projects are often carried out by financial institutions, data scientists, or analysts to assess the credit risk associated with their customers and make informed decisions on lending, credit limits, and collection strategies. These projects are critical for financial institutions to manage credit risk effectively, minimize losses from defaults, and make responsible lending decisions. They rely on the power of data analysis and machine learning to make predictions based on historical data and customer behavior patterns.
